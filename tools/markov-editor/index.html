Match Flow Editor

Each circle on the pitch is a moment in a match — like "midfielder has the ball" or "shot on target." The arrows show what can happen next and how likely it is.

To explore: Click any circle to see where the ball can go from there. Then click a second circle to see exactly how the game flows between those two moments.

You can also drag circles to rearrange the layout, or hover over arrows to see probabilities.
